Highlights of Billy Graham's Life and Ministry

  • 1918: Born in North Carolina
  • 1934: Became saved in a Mordecai Ham meeting.
  • 1939: ordained by Peniel Baptist Church in Palatka, Florida.
  • 1943: Married Ruth Bell, Together they had 5 children and were married for 63 years.
  • 1947: 6000 people attended his first crusade held in Grand Rapids, Michigan.
  • 1947: Became the president of Northwest Bible College at age 30, At the time was the youngest person to serve as a college or university president.
  • 1950: Billy Graham Evangelistic Association is founded.
  • 1963: Year he posted bail for Martin Luther King Jr. after he was arrested in Birmingham, Alabama.
  • 1973: Year he held his first crusade in South Africa and denounced apartheid. He corresponded with Nelson Mandela during the latterâ€™s 27-year prison sentence.
  • 1991: Holds Crusade in New York City's Central Park, 250,000 people attended, his largest North American event.
  • 2005: His last official crusade was in New York City.
  • 2013: on his 95th birthday, his last sermon "My Hope America" was broadcast on televisionm stations across the country.
  • February 21st, 2018, Billy Graham passes away at the age of 99.
  • An estimated 2.2 Billion people have heard Billy Graham preach.
  • An estimated 215 Million people heard him preach live.
  • An estimated 2.2 Million people made the decision to become a Christian at his crusades.
  • Estimated that Billy Graham preached in 185 nations or territories
